<h2 id="contact">Contact</h2>

Email [hi@tasfer.app](mailto:hi@tasfer.app) for help with app issues, general
feedback, or feature requests. You can also ask a public question in [GitHub
Discussions](https://github.com/tasferlabs/tasfer/discussions).

<h2 id="troubleshooting">Troubleshooting</h2>

Start with [Troubleshooting & FAQ](/docs/app/troubleshooting) for common issues.
If possible, export a current backup from **Settings → Data → Export all** before
trying recovery steps.

<h2 id="report-a-bug">Report a bug</h2>

Open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/tasferlabs/tasfer/issues) and include:

- your device and operating-system version
- the Tasfer app version
- steps that reproduce the problem
- what you expected and what happened instead

<Callout kind="note" title="Keep private documents private.">
  Do not attach notes or screenshots containing sensitive information to a
  public issue. Email us instead if the report requires private context.
</Callout>

<h2 id="privacy">Privacy</h2>

Read [Privacy & data](/docs/app/privacy) for details about local storage,
encrypted sync, network access, and diagnostics.
