  The Tasfer editor packages are MIT-licensed source. A supported public SDK is
  on our roadmap, but the packages are not published yet.

The editor engine is currently developed as part of the Tasfer application. We
are keeping its internal package boundaries while the product and APIs settle,
without making compatibility or support promises to external consumers. Their
source remains available under the MIT License in the meantime.

Follow [project updates](https://github.com/tasferlabs/tasfer) for news about its
availability.
